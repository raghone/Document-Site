Overview

The **Data Backup** feature in XFilesPro ensures that your critical data is securely stored and easily recoverable in case of accidental loss, system failure, or cyber threats. With automated backups and multiple storage options, your data remains protected at all times.

Why Data Backup?

Backing up your data is essential for:

  • ● **Preventing Data Loss** due to hardware failure or human error.
  • ● **Ensuring Business Continuity** in case of cyberattacks.
  • ● **Meeting Compliance Standards** (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.).
  • ● **Reducing Downtime & Recovery Costs**.

Key Features

  • ● **Automated Backup Scheduling** – Set up daily, weekly, or custom backups.
  • ● **Multi-Cloud Backup Support** – Store backups on AWS, Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.
  • ● **Incremental & Full Backups** – Optimize storage and backup speed.
  • ● **Versioning & Retention Policies** – Restore previous versions of files.

Backup Types

XFilesPro supports multiple backup methods:

  • ● **Full Backup** – Copies all data for complete recovery.
  • ● **Incremental Backup** – Saves only changes made since the last backup.
  • ● **Differential Backup** – Backs up all changes since the last full backup.
  • ● **Cloud & On-Premise Backup** – Choose where to store your backups.

Automated Backup

With **automated scheduling**, you can:

  • ● **Set & Forget** – Configure backups once, and let the system handle the rest.
  • ● **Customize Frequency** – Daily, weekly, or monthly backup options.
  • ● **Receive Notifications** – Get alerts on backup status and failures.

Security & Encryption

Your backups are protected with:

  • ● **AES-256 Encryption** – Industry-leading encryption for secure storage.
  • ● **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** – Prevent unauthorized access.
  • ● **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** – Define access permissions.
  • ● **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)** – Secure data during transfer.

Data Recovery

Quickly restore lost data with:

  • ● **One-Click Recovery** – Restore files instantly.
  • ● **Granular Restore** – Recover specific files or entire databases.
  • ● **Disaster Recovery Solutions** – Minimize downtime after unexpected failures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I back up my data?

A: It depends on your data usage, but we recommend daily or weekly backups.

Q: Can I store backups on both cloud and local storage?

A: Yes! XFilesPro supports hybrid storage options.

Q: Is my backed-up data encrypted?

A: Yes, all backups are encrypted using AES-256 security.
