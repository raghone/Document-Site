Overview

XFilesPro’s **Document Generation** feature simplifies the creation of business documents by automating workflows, ensuring accuracy, and integrating seamlessly with various data sources.

With powerful **template management, real-time customization, and multi-format support**, XFilesPro allows you to generate invoices, contracts, reports, and more within seconds.

Key Features

  • ● **Automated Document Creation** – Reduce manual effort with pre-set rules.
  • ● **Dynamic Data Insertion** – Populate documents with real-time data from CRM/ERP.
  • ● **Batch Processing** – Generate multiple documents at once.
  • ● **Cloud & On-Premise Storage** – Securely store generated documents.

Automation & Customization

Save time with **workflow automation** and **customizable document rules**:

  • ● **Trigger-Based Generation** – Auto-generate documents based on predefined conditions.
  • ● **Conditional Formatting** – Personalize content dynamically based on user data.
  • ● **Scheduled Document Creation** – Automate recurring reports and invoices.

Pre-Built & Custom Templates

Choose from **a library of ready-made templates** or create custom designs:

  • ● **Business Contracts**
  • ● **Invoices & Billing Statements**
  • ● **Sales & Purchase Orders**
  • ● **HR & Employee Documents**

Supported Formats

XFilesPro supports multiple output formats, including:

  • ● **PDF** – Ideal for contracts, reports, and official documents.
  • ● **Word (.docx)** – Easily editable document format.
  • ● **Excel (.xlsx)** – Generate structured data reports.
  • ● **HTML & XML** – Web and database integration.

Security & Compliance

Ensure **data privacy and document integrity** with:

  • ● **End-to-End Encryption** – Secure data transfer and storage.
  • ● **Role-Based Access Control** – Restrict access to sensitive documents.
  • ● **Audit Trails & Version Control** – Track document changes and history.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I integrate document generation with my CRM?

A: Yes, XFilesPro supports seamless integration with CRM and ERP systems for automated document generation.

Q: Can I customize document layouts?

A: Absolutely! You can use pre-built templates or design your own with drag-and-drop customization.
