Overview

**Extract** is an advanced document processing tool that enables users to extract relevant data from files, emails, and cloud storage automatically. It simplifies data retrieval by leveraging AI-powered text recognition and structured data extraction.

Key Features

  • ● **AI-Powered Data Extraction** – Extracts key information from PDFs, images, and documents.
  • ● **OCR Technology** – Converts scanned documents into editable text.
  • ● **Automated Data Classification** – Categorizes extracted data for better organization.
  • ● **Cloud & Local File Extraction** – Works with Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and local files.
  • ● **Seamless Integration** – Connects with CRMs, databases, and other third-party tools.

How It Works

Using **Extract** is simple and efficient. Follow these steps:

  1. ● **Upload Your Document** – Choose a PDF, image, or text file.
  2. ● **Select Extraction Type** – Define what data needs to be extracted (e.g., names, dates, numbers).
  3. ● **Run Extraction Process** – The AI-powered engine will analyze and extract data.
  4. ● **Download or Export** – Save extracted data or export it to your preferred format.

Use Cases

**Extract** can be used in various industries, including:

  • ● **Finance** – Extract financial data from invoices, bank statements, and reports.
  • ● **Healthcare** – Retrieve patient details and medical records from scanned documents.
  • ● **Legal** – Process contracts, agreements, and legal documents efficiently.
  • ● **Retail & E-commerce** – Extract order details and invoices from receipts.
  • ● **Education** – Automate data collection from research papers and student records.

Integration

Connect **Extract** with your existing tools:

  • ● **CRM Integration** – Sync extracted data with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho.
  • ● **Cloud Storage** – Automatically process documents from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive.
  • ● **Database Export** – Store structured data in SQL or NoSQL databases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What file formats does Extract support?

A: Extract supports PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX, and TXT file formats.

Q: Can I automate data extraction?

A: Yes, you can set up automated workflows for scheduled data extraction.

Q: Is Extract compatible with cloud storage?

A: Yes, it integrates with Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, and more.
